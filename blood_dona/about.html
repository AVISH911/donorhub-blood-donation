Our Story

How DonorHub began and our journey so far

DonorHub wasn't born in a boardroom, It was born from a shared realization. We (Avish, Sahil, and Yash) witnessed the anxiety and chaos families face when searching for a blood donor during an emergency. We saw a critical problem: in a world of instant connectivity, why is it so hard to find a blood donor?

This shared concern ignited a spark. We combined our diverse skills and shared passion for technology to build a solution. What started as a project to address a real-world problem has now evolved into a comprehensive platform connecting donors, recipients, and blood banks in real-time.

DonorHub represents our dedication to using technology for social good. Every line of code is written with the goal of creating a tangible, positive impact on society and saving lives.

Our Mission & Vision

What drives us forward every day

Our Mission

To eliminate blood shortages by creating a reliable network of voluntary blood donors and facilitating timely blood availability during emergencies through our comprehensive digital platform.

Our Vision

A world where no one dies due to unavailability of blood, with a self-sufficient network of regular voluntary donors supporting the healthcare system and creating a culture of regular blood donation.

Our Technology

Built with modern MERN stack technology, DonorHub features real-time notifications, secure authentication, and a responsive design that works seamlessly across all devices.

Meet Our Team

The passionate developers behind DonorHub

Avish

Full-Stack Developer & System Architect

As the technical backbone of DonorHub, Avish engineered the entire platform infrastructure with expertise in:

  • Backend Development: Node.js & Express.js server architecture
  • Database Design: MongoDB schema design and optimization
  • API Development: RESTful API endpoints and integration
  • Email System: OTP verification and notification services
  • Frontend Pages: HTML structure and page routing
  • Business Logic: Core application functionality and workflows
  • Market Research: Analysis of existing blood donation systems

His comprehensive approach ensured a robust, scalable, and efficient platform from database to user interface.

Sahil

Authentication Specialist & Security Lead

Sahil implemented the comprehensive security framework that protects DonorHub users and their data:

  • User Authentication: Secure login/registration system
  • Session Management: Persistent user sessions across pages
  • Route Protection: Page access control and authorization
  • Security Protocols: Data encryption and protection measures
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions and restrictions

His work ensures that user data remains secure while providing seamless navigation across the platform.

Yash

Project Visionary & Research Lead

Yash provided the creative direction and foundational research that shaped DonorHub:

  • Project Conception: Initial idea and problem identification
  • Reference Collection: Gathering best practices and case studies
  • Feature Planning: User requirement analysis and feature ideation
  • Industry Insights: Healthcare system research and compliance

His vision and research laid the groundwork for a platform that truly addresses real-world blood donation challenges.
